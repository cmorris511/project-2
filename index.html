Hiking across the U.S. takes you on an adventure that allows you to explore some of the most breathtaking scenery in the world. Whatever trails you prefer, there is a hike for everyone. This site showcases some of the best hikes you can experience in the U.S.

Featured Trails

Appalachian Trail

The Appalachian Trail is a large adventure that stretches over 2,000 miles from Georgia to Maine, passing through forests, mountains, and towns. It’s a classic American hiking experience for any and everyone.

Learn More

Grand Canyon National Park

The Grand Canyon offers jawdropping views and trails that range from short walks to challenging hikes. The Bright Angel Trail is a must try for the best views of the canyon walls and Colorado River.

Learn More

Yosemite National Park

Yosemite is famous for its granite cliffs, waterfalls, and giant sequoias. Trails like the Mist Trail to Vernal and Nevada Falls offer a perfect mix of challenge and breathtaking scenery.
